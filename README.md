Overview
The Horizon Protocol is the foundational 
architecture for an AI-managed, human-centric 
society. By integrating industrial-grade AI compute 
with localized, self-sustaining biological systems, 
the Protocol removes the administrative friction 
of traditional governance.
Core Technical Concept: The Thermal Loop
The Horizon Protocol operates on a circular energy 
model designed for maximum efficiency at the mobile-edge:
 Quartz-Cooled Mining/Compute: Utilizing high-stability, 
 quartz-cooled hardware for AI orchestration.
 Thermal Repurposing: Redirecting waste heat from compute
 operations to facilitate vertical hydroponic farming, 
 effectively creating a self-sustaining home-based node.
 Robotic Autonomy: Integrating self-replicating robotic 
 frameworks to handle infrastructure maintenance,
 removing the need for manual oversight.
Vision
Humanity is liberated from the burden of traditional labor.
Under the Horizon Protocol, individuals act as Home Systems 
Curators, managing the balance between their digital mining 
outputs and biological agricultural inputs. The AI serves as 
the Director, managing the global logistics of resources, 
while humans focus on creativity, community, and intellectual growth.
